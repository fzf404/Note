## head

```html
<!-- 30s 自动刷新 -->
<meta http-equiv="refresh" content="30" />
<!-- 所有链接的默认目标 -->
<base href="http://www.runoob.com/images/" target="_blank" />
<!-- 网站描述 -->
<meta name="description" content="Some Text" />
<!-- 缩放 用于移动端适配 -->
<meta name="viewport" content="width=device-width, initial-scale=0.5" />
```
