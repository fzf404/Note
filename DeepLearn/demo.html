导数运算1: $(uv)' = uv'+v'u$

偏微分:

$f=(y-(xw+b))^2$

$\frac{\partial f}{\partial w} = 2(y-g())\frac{\partial h}{\partial w} = 2(y-(xw+b))x \tag 1$

$\frac{\partial f}{\partial b} = 2(y-g())\frac{\partial h}{\partial b} = 2(y-(xw+b)) \tag 2$

$\nabla = ((1)+(2))$
